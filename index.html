About BLADO

BLADO is a Nepali clothing house inspired by the elegance of old money and timeless dressing. We believe true luxury is quiet — found not in logos, but in craftsmanship, quality, and character.

Every piece we create reflects a sense of heritage and understated sophistication. BLADO is for those who value tradition, carry themselves with dignity, and invest in garments that last a lifetime.

Rooted in Kathmandu and tailored for the world, BLADO is more than clothing — it's a symbol of who you are when no one is watching.
